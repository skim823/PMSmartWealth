📈 EPF Members Investment Scheme (EPF-MIS)

Akaun Persaraan

Qualified members can enhance asset allocation metrics by routing up to 30% of the value exceeding their required Basic Savings out of their EPF Akaun Persaraan into approved global equity growth vehicles.

Strategic Benefits for Local Investors:

  • ✓ Zero Cash Outlay: Build global tech exposure without restricting your current monthly household cash flow or liquid safety reserves.
  • ✓ Capture International Alpha: Shift a portion of domestic exposure into mega-cap technological drivers (Nvidia, Apple, Microsoft) to maximize compounding potential.

🛡️ Private Retirement Scheme (PRS) Advantages

Tax Advantage

A dedicated corporate retirement program providing an independent wealth framework alongside your statutory funds, structured with critical investor protection benefits.

Premium Plan Takeaways:

  • ✓ Free Personal Accident (PA) Insurance: Investors maintaining a minimum PRS net asset value (NAV) of **RM5,000** automatically qualify for complimentary group personal accident coverage (Ages 18 to 59), including accidental death or permanent disablement protections.
  • ✓ RM3,000 Annual Personal Tax Relief: Claim an immediate individual income tax relief deduction of up to RM3,000 per year, instantly reducing your highest taxable income bracket.
  • ✓ Institutional Privilege Circle: Contributions pile up toward your Mutual Gold, Elite, or Platinum qualification milestones for added lifestyle rewards.
